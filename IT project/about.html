Our Story

Founded in 2005, Hayah Clinic has grown from a small community hospital to a leading healthcare institution serving patients across the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide exceptional medical care to every patient who walks through our doors.

Today, we're recognized as a pioneer in patient-centered care, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate service. Our team of world-class physicians, nurses, and healthcare professionals work together to deliver the best possible outcomes for our patients.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to treating each patient as an individual, not just a case number. We take the time to listen, understand, and develop personalized treatment plans that address not just physical symptoms, but emotional and spiritual needs as well.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To inspire hope and contribute to health and well-being by providing the best care to every patient through integrated clinical practice, education, and research.

Our Vision

To transform medicine through innovation, quality care, and service to humanity, becoming the most trusted partner for life in health and healing.

Our Values

Compassion, Excellence, Integrity, Teamwork, Innovation, and Stewardship guide every decision we make and every interaction we have with our patients.

Our Facilities

Hayah Clinic's state-of-the-art facilities are designed with patient comfort and healing in mind. Our main campus spans over 1 million square feet, featuring:

  • Advanced surgical suites with robotic technology
  • Comprehensive cancer treatment center
  • 24/7 emergency department with Level I trauma center
  • Modern patient rooms with family accommodations
  • Healing gardens and meditation spaces

We continuously invest in the latest medical technologies and facility upgrades to ensure our patients receive care in the most advanced healing environment possible.
